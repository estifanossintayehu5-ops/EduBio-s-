About Me

My name is Amina. I was born and raised in Saudi Arabia. Later, my family and I moved to Tigray around the middle of 2019, and we lived there for about five to six years. Now, we are living in Addis Ababa, and soon it will be one year since we moved here. I am a grade 11 student. Moving from one country to another many times is not easy because every time you have to start over and get used to new people and a new life. However, I always try to stay positive and thankful for everything. I am not sad about my experiences. In fact, I thank God for every place I lived in because each place gave me beautiful memories and important lessons. I lived some of the happiest days of my life, and also some difficult days, but now all of them became memories that I will never forget. These days, I am focusing on improving myself and becoming a better person. One of my dreams is to open my own business in the future, and I hope I can achieve that someday. I love my family very much, and I also have amazing friends who are very special to me. Even though we live in different countries now, they still mean a lot to me.
