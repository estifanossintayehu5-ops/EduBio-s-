About Me

My name is kalkidan Tadese. I was born in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, in 2000 E.C. I grew up in a loving and caring family. Since my childhood, I have loved education very much, especially trying new things, which always made me happy. I attended my primary education at a school near my home. I always worked hard to achieve good academic results, and my teachers encouraged me a lot. Besides my studies, I also participated in sports activities organized at school. Currently, I am a grade 11 student. In my free time, I enjoy watching movies, listening to gospel songs, and spending time with my friends. I also enjoy trying and learning new things because they help me improve myself. Whenever I face difficulties in life, I try to overcome them with patience. My family always supports and advises me, and they have a very important place in my life. In the future, I want to become an educated and helpful person to others. I am working hard to achieve my dreams. I believe in myself and always try to have a positive attitude. I believe that I will achieve many good things in my future life.
