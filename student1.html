About Me

I also attended school well, especially trying new things always made me happy. I attended primary school at New Hope School near Bethel. I always worked hard to get good grades, and my teachers encouraged me a lot. Besides my studies, I serve the church. I am currently a 11th grade student and I am studying at Karaneyo High School. Whenever I face problems in my life, I try to overcome them with patience. My family always supports and advises me and has a very important place in my life. In the future, I want to be an educated and helpful person. I am working hard to achieve my dream. I believe in myself and I always try to have a positive attitude. I believe that I will achieve many good things in my future life.
